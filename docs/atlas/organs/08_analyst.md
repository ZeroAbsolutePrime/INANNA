# INNER ORGAN · ANALYST
## The Reasoner — Structured Analysis, Decomposition, and Evaluation

**Ring: Inner AI Organs**
**Grade: D (named and referenced, not independently built)**
**Version: 1.0 · Date: 2026-04-24**

---

## Identity

**What it is:**
ANALYST is the reasoning organ of INANNA NYX.
Where CROWN generates responses and NAMMU routes intent,
ANALYST decomposes complex problems, evaluates options,
and produces structured reasoning.

**What it does (intended):**
- Breaks complex requests into structured sub-tasks
- Evaluates multiple options and their trade-offs
- Produces analytical output in structured format
- Supports decision-making without replacing human judgment
- Reasons about system health and anomalies

**What it must never do:**
- Make decisions for the operator
- Replace human judgment on complex or sensitive matters
- Produce analysis without transparency about its reasoning

---

## Ring

**Inner AI Organs** — ANALYST is a separate reasoning thread.
CROWN speaks. ANALYST thinks.
They are designed to use different model weights or prompts
to separate generation from structured reasoning.

## Mission

ANALYST exists because generation and reasoning are different tasks.

CROWN generates conversational responses.
ANALYST should produce structured, decomposed, evaluative output:

```
Request: "Should I reply to Matxalen now or wait?"
ANALYST output (intended):
  FACTORS:
    - Her email was sent 18 hours ago (time sensitivity: medium)
    - You have a meeting with her team tomorrow (context: relevant)
    - Your last reply was 3 days ago (pattern: you tend to reply quickly)
  OPTIONS:
    A. Reply now — preserves relationship rhythm
    B. Wait until after meeting — more context for reply
  RECOMMENDATION: Reply now with brief note, fuller response after meeting
  CONFIDENCE: 0.72
```

This kind of output is fundamentally different from CROWN's narrative voice.
It requires a separate prompt, a structured output format, and ideally
a different model focused on reasoning rather than generation.

## Current State

### What Is Present (minimally)

- `FacultyMonitor` tracks call latency per faculty
- `core/orchestration.py` has scaffolding
- CROWN responds to analytical questions using general generation

### What Is Entirely Missing

- Dedicated `core/analyst.py` module
- Separate ANALYST prompt optimized for structured reasoning
- Structured output format (JSON or markdown with defined schema)
- ANALYST as a separate callable from OPERATOR
- Integration with decision-support (Cycle 10+)

---

## Desired Function

A proper ANALYST organ would:
- Accept a structured question or scenario
- Produce factors, options, recommendation, confidence score
- Use a reasoning-optimized model or prompt
- Feed structured output to CROWN for natural presentation
- Log analytical decisions for review

In civic deployment, ANALYST would provide:
- Impact analysis for proposed decisions
- Resource conflict detection
- Timeline and dependency mapping
- Risk assessment with confidence intervals

## Evaluation

**Grade: D**

ANALYST is the most underbuilt of the ten core organs.
It exists as a name and a concept but has no real implementation.

Single most important gap:
**ANALYST does not exist as a callable module.**

Priority for future agent: create `core/analyst.py`
with a dedicated reasoning prompt, structured output format,
and integration into the dispatch chain as a separate organ.

This is one of the most valuable things a future agent could build.
On a 70B model with good reasoning capability,
a proper ANALYST transforms INANNA from a capable assistant
into a genuine decision-support system.
