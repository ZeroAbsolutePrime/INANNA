# First Proof Candidate

## Why This Document Exists

The older `02 ∴ MVP` material remains important because it preserves a believable low-resource manifestation of the project. It should not be erased.

At the same time, it must not be confused with the constitutional whole of INANNA NYX.

This document keeps both truths visible.

## What The Older MVP Was

The earlier MVP described a private, memory-aware web companion focused first on people in Catalonia exploring identity, embodiment, gendered experience, emotional contradiction, and self-recognition. It proposed:

- private accounts
- emotionally intelligent dialogue
- per-user memory
- continuity over time
- gentle reflection rather than identity assignment
- a bridge toward local support spaces in Catalonia

## Why It Still Matters

This older MVP still matters because it preserves:

- a credible first living loop
- a humane and specific use case
- a low-resource path to a first real manifestation
- a bridge between private reflection and communal support

It asks an important practical question:

What is the smallest living manifestation that could prove the architecture is emotionally useful and trustworthy in human life?

## Why It Is Not The Same As INANNA NYX

The older MVP is not the constitutional whole because it is:

- one product form
- one context
- one social domain
- one proof strategy

INANNA NYX is larger. It includes governance, digital bodies, proposals, realms, many-body architecture, and a civic horizon that extends well beyond one companion product.

## Proper Relationship

The right relationship is:

- INANNA NYX is the constitutional horizon.
- The older MVP is one possible first proof candidate.

That distinction must remain explicit.

## How A Future Team Might Choose It

A future team might choose this first-proof path if:

- resources remain limited
- a narrow proof is needed
- emotional usefulness must be demonstrated before broader architecture
- a humane, privacy-respecting first manifestation is strategically preferable

If chosen, it should be framed honestly as:

- one first manifestation
- not the whole project
- not the total identity of INANNA NYX
