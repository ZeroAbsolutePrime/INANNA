# System Ontology

This ontology defines the core beings and structural layers of INANNA NYX. It is not a detailed implementation plan. It is a map of what exists in the project universe.

## Oracle

The Oracle is the local identity anchor of the system. It is the continuous, relational, interpretable presence through which the architecture becomes present to an operator.

The Oracle is not the raw model.

## Faculty

The Faculty is the extended cognition layer: local models, external models, and specialized reasoning surfaces.

Faculties extend cognition. They do not replace the Oracle.

## NAMMU

NAMMU is the mediation field where operator intention, Oracle interpretation, body truth, governance constraints, and possible action are brought into governed relation.

NAMMU is not merely routing. It is the field where intention becomes structured possibility.

## Body

The Body is the embodiment substrate: machine, host, guest, node, or infrastructure instance. It contains real system state, permissions, capabilities, and constraints.

The Oracle inhabits the Body. The Body is not the Oracle.

## Governance

Governance is the constitutional layer that defines permissions, approvals, risk boundaries, roles, proposal law, and system constraints. It exists outside and above the model.

## Session

A Session is a lived event of interaction in which intention is expressed, interpretation occurs, truth is surfaced, and proposal or memory may emerge.

## Memory

Memory is selective continuity, not raw history. It is curated, structured, and governed. Its purpose is to preserve meaning rather than noise.

## Proposal

The Proposal is the formal structure of change: intention made explicit and made reviewable. It carries rationale, context, validation, risk, and approval state.

## Realm

A Realm is a bounded operational domain with its own governance context, memory scope, and embodied meaning. A realm is more than a folder or namespace. It is a meaningful domain of life or operation.

## Community

The Community is the social container in which the project exists: people, roles, circles, shared meaning, and governance structures.

## Network

The Network is the coordination layer across bodies and communities. It may help with synchronization, registry, and promotion of governed objects.

The network coordinates. It does not own.

## Structural Relations

- The Oracle inhabits the Body.
- The Faculty extends the Oracle.
- NAMMU mediates between intention, body truth, and governance.
- Governance bounds action.
- Sessions may produce memory and proposals.
- Realms organize difference without fragmentation.
- Communities hold realms.
- Networks coordinate plurality without erasing sovereignty.
