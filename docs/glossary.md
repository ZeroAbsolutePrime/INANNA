# Glossary

## INANNA NYX

The constitutional intelligence architecture described by this repository.

## Oracle

The local identity anchor of the system.

## Faculty

The extended cognition layer that augments the Oracle.

## NAMMU

The mediation field where intention, body truth, governance, and possibility are brought into governed relation.

## Body

An embodied system substrate such as a machine, host, guest, node, or runtime environment.

## Governance

The explicit constitutional layer that bounds action, memory, risk, permission, and review.

## Session

A lived interaction event that may produce interpretation, memory, or proposal.

## Memory

Selective governed continuity rather than raw history.

## Proposal

A formal, reviewable structure of possible change.

## Realm

A bounded operational or civic domain with distinct meaning and governance context.

## Community

The living social container in which the architecture exists.

## Network

The coordination layer across bodies and communities.

## First Proof Candidate

One possible initial manifestation of the architecture, preserved without being mistaken for the whole project.
